### Voice Powers Business Productivity - Voice is a natural, more efficient way to perform tasks. - Voice is about to amplify business productivity in a big way. - Business relies on communications and conversation - When you add a vocal or conversational layer on top of your CRM - You power an entirely new user experience that enables you to get more data in and more intelligence out — in a fast, natural, and real-time way. - More data available for analysis, the better and more accurate your insights and predictions are.
