What is Covid-19 virus?

- In COVID-19, 'CO' stands for 'corona,' 'VI' for 'virus,' and 'D' for disease. - Formerly, this disease was referred to as “2019 novel coronavirus” or “2019-nCoV”. - There are many types of human coronaviruses including some that commonly cause mild upper-respiratory tract illnesses. - Researchers have used cryogenic electron microscopy to show that coronaviruses enter human cells through an interaction with angiotensin-converting enzyme 2 (ACE2). ![covid](https://dp9bxf2pat5uz.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/coronavirus-targets-image-750x500.jpg)
