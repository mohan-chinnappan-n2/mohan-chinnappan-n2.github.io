- As business becomes more global, your users are accessing your data through Salesforce from **all over the world**. Users want information faster and available at all times. Salesforce Edge Network delivers a consistent user experience **regardless of a user's location**. ### Salesforce Edge Network delivers - an enhanced user experience - consistent quality content delivery, - improved download times for users from across the globe. ### How it does this? - By redirecting requests to the **closest Salesforce location** where **Salesforce Edge Network** is deployed. - It provides a range of services like: - TLS termination - Static Caching - TCP optimizations. - The application delivers data using the Salesforce infrastructure which protects, uses, and processes data appropriately and in accordance with the law. - With **Salesforce Edge Network**, you can continue to get the same trusted infrastructure while also receiving **these improved benefits**.
- Salesforce Edge Network brings Salesforce Services **closer to the user** on the trusted Salesforce infrastructure. It ensures requests land sooner to the trusted Salesforce infrastructure and can take advantage of the caching and transport optimizations that Salesforce Edge Network provides on the dedicated high-bandwidth links between Salesforce locations.
