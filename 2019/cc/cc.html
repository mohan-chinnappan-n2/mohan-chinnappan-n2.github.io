    6. Customer Experience Expectations

    • Today’s customers are more tech-savvy and their expectations are different. They demand a new commitment to Customer Experience (CX) and game-changing innovations in Financial Services. Half of the world’s population will be digital natives by 2020 and they have never lived in a non-digital world. Each connected customer owns around 3 connected devices on average. It is estimated that US adults will spend around 3 hours a day using a mobile device this year. Their financial services experiences shouldn’t force them to relive their parents’ non-digital world. They expect gamification for the learning and engagement that makes financial services activities more fun. They expect more relevant, engaging, welcoming and effective digital experiences. They do not want to repeat who they are and what they want when serviced.

    Customer Engagement

    • Lets customers share experiences and expertise with other customers (peers) with the aim of deflecting cases away from support
    • Provide access to their data (accounts, service cases,...)
    • Aid in their purchasing decision by providing information about your company and products: up-selling and cross-selling
    • Customers can post questions to a user forum (powered on the backend by Chatter), view knowledge articles (Salesforce knowledge)
    • Customers can have live chat with the Snap-Ins Chat component in Community Builder

    Customer Engagement - Measuring success

    • Number of cases deflected because of the community
    • Reduciton in Time spent by a support agent on each case
    • CSAT: Customer satisfaction
    • Net promoter score (NPS) - gauge the loyalty of a firm's customer relationships.
